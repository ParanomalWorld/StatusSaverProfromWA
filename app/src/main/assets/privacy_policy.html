Privacy Policy

This privacy policy governs the manner in which our app collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the Status Saver Pro app ("App") developed by Divine Engine.

Personal identification information

We do not collect any personal identification information from Users.

Non-personal identification information

We may collect non-personal identification information about Users whenever they interact with our App. Non-personal identification information may include the type of device and technical information about Users' means of connection to our App, such as the operating system and the Internet service providers utilized and other similar information.

Storage permission

Our App requires access to the device's storage to save and access status updates. We do not access any other data or files stored on the device.

User permissions

Our App requires the following permissions from Users:

  • Storage: To save and access status updates on the device.
  • Save, Share, and Delete: Users can save, share, and delete status updates within the App.

- Internet Access: The app requires internet access to download status updates.

- Post Notifications: The app uses this permission to display notifications for new status updates.

- Write External Storage: The app needs access to external storage to save and access status updates.

- Read External Storage: The app requires access to read external storage to access status updates saved on the device.
